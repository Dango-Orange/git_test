Welcome to Midgar!



  Midgar is the most technologically advanced city on the planet. Its large circular structure is supported above the ground by eight mako reactors and a central pillar, with various other supports around the city. On top of the structure lies the city proper divided into eight sectors. Sectors 1 through 8 refer to the eight districts of the city, with Sector 0 being the central district that holds the main headquarters of the Shinra Electric Power Company. It is the most densley populated city in the world and also boasts the strongest econonomy of all major cities. Midgar is a global cultural, financial, high-tech, entertainment, and media center with a significant influence on commerce, health care, and scientific output. It is sometimes described as the capital of the world.



History


  • EYA 1971 - First year of Midgar construction. After a Mako hotspot is discovered, Shinra begins work by building a wall around the area's perimeter to ensure safe and efficient construction. Construction starts on Mako Reactor 0 to supply energy for the project.

  • EYA 1972 - Mako Reactor 0 becomes operational in the center of the contruction area.

  • EYA 1974 - The Midgar perimeter and Mako Reactor 1 are completed. Sped up by the operation of Mako Reactor 0, construction concentrates on ensuring safety of workers and generation of energy. More ground is leveled, leaving behind rocks and parts of foundation that were difficult to move. This leads to an influx of workers and the establishment of lodgings that would become the basis of the Midgar Undercity.

  • EYA 1975 - The Midgar train system and Sector 1 upper plate are completed. The train system facilitates more effectient movement of goods and people, accelerating construction of additional portions of the upper plate. Mako Reactors 2 and 3 are completed.

  • EYA 1976 - Mako Reactors 4 through 8 are completed and migration to the upper plate is in full swing, supported by the bountiful supply of energy being generated.

  • EYA 1977 - Construction of Midgar is complete and Shinra relocates headquarters to the center of Sector 0. The population of the city increases rapidly as a result of large scale efforts to attract immigration and the cities abundance of opportunity.


Industry



  Midgar's primary mode of industry is as an energy supplier through the Shinra Electric Power Company's Mako Reactors. These reactors handle both the extraction of Mako energy from deep within the earths core and the distrubution of energy to their surrounding sectors and to neighboring towns and villages. The maintenance and operation of these reactors is the single biggest provider of employment throughout the city of Midgar and is the largest source of its economy.



  In addition, Midgar is the worlds hub of manufacturing and science. Through sub-divisions of Shinra Inc, it is the center of weapons development and manufacturing, motor mobile manufacturing and distribution, genetic engineering and research, space exploration and aeronautics, and food and pharmaceutical production. Due to this, it is home to the greatest scientists, engineers, and thinkers in the world.

Residential



  The Sector 7 Shinra Employee Housing Area (pictured above) is a suburban residential area comprising perfectly planned suburbs where Shinra corporate employees are located. It is a town comprised of stone and wooden houses and larger apartment buildings along its roads. The town is located behind walls and beneath large bridges that connect to the spectacular Shinra Building, which can be seen clearly from the town. As with other topside sectors of Midgar, Sector 7 is a residential district for the middle and upper classes. It has a high standard of living, and a carefully managed population with balanced age demographics. While all Sectors provide a high quality of life, Sector 7 stands out in particular as it is a designated area for Shinra employees, where employees appear to be assigned houses, and military members can sign up for a house in the sector through a lottery system.



Politics



  The city of Midgar has been a democracy since its inception. It is currently run by Mayor Domino who, along with Deputy Mayor Hart, works in close association with the Shinra corporation to govern the city. Though they weild no actual political power, Mayor Domino allocates many sectors of governing to Shinra associates who assist the Mayor in bringing his vision for Midgar to life with funding, staff, and their pre-established infrastructure throughout the city. His primary associates within the company are: Head of Public Safety, Heidegger, who is in charge of local security forces, and Head of Urban Development, Reeve, who manages city planning and construction. The mayors office currently resides within the Shinra building at the center of the city.



Transportation



  The primary mode of transportation throughout Midgar is actually one of the cities oldest infrastructures: an extensive railway system. Each sector of Midgar has its own train station, making a total of eight stations used by passengers to embark and disembark at various points in the city proper. The system operates on Midgar Standard Time, and is run through the city's upper plate and linked to the Undercity via a giant cirular track that spirals around the city's central pillar known as the "Corkscrew Tunnel". These rail systems are heavily monitored by the city's security system, with security checkpoints along the routes used to automatically scan the passenger's ID cards to ensure the safety of all riders. Train services operate from 5:13 am to 1:35 am.



  On the Upper Plate there is also a sprawling highway system which is used primarily for local travel and delivery of goods and services. Although not as fast as the railway system, some citizens still prefer to use them for the scenic cityscape views that can only be seen from up above Midgar. Security forces also use these highways to transport military personal and equipment to all sectors to ensure the safety of their citizens.
